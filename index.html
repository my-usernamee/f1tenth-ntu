What is NTU DeepSpeed?

DeepSpeed is NTU's student autonomous racing effort: a place to build compact race cars, test algorithms on real hardware, and learn how robotics systems behave when speed matters.

02

Why people should care

Autonomous racing turns self-driving research into a fast feedback loop. Students can safely push perception, planning, and controls on a smaller platform before ideas move toward larger vehicles.

03

How they can join

Join as a coder, builder, designer, strategist, organizer, or curious beginner. We will add the real sign-up form and club channels once they are ready.
