About

A hands-on space for robotics and racing.

F1TENTH NTU brings students together to work on autonomous racing cars using perception, localization, planning, and control. Whether you are new to robotics or already comfortable with code and hardware, there is a place to contribute.

01

Robotics

Build and tune the vehicle platform, sensors, and compute stack.

02

Autonomy

Work on perception, mapping, planning, simulation, and controls.

03

Racing

Test algorithms on track and improve lap time through iteration.
